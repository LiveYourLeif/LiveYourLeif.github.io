About Me

I have a background in Medialogy from Aalborg University (AAU), which is a computer science degree focused on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), software development for applications, VR, games, and other emerging technologies. I'm currently in my final semester of a Master's in Cyber Security at AAU, where I've explored various areas of cybersecurity.

My shift from Medialogy to Cyber Security was driven by a growing fascination with digital footprinting and how software can be exploited. I became intrigued by the psychological aspects of deception technologies used in phishing, and how adversaries use malware, drive-by downloads, and other methods to cause real-world harm to individuals and organizations. This sparked my deep interest in analyzing such threats and the tactics behind them.

Alongside my studies, I work as a Junior Analyst at Dubex A/S in their Cyber Defence Center, focused on Managed Detection and Response (MDR), where I apply many of the skills I've learned to real-world scenarios.
